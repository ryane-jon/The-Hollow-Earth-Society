The Gloperjocker

A truly terrifying being, covered up by the global elites for thousands of years. The Gloperjocker was rarely spoken of outside of the Bilderberg meetings until its existence was brought to light by the conspiracy theory society in early 2023.

It is thought that the Gloperjocker escaped from the hollow earth in 79 AD and subsequently caused the eruption of Mount Vesuvius. Its unstoppable rampages continued for 1100 years until the Red Winter of 1179, during which the Scots barely managed to trap it. The Bridge of Dee was then constructed aroud it, and Aberdeen was built in order to keep a watchful eye over the Gloperjocker.

However, in a terrifying turn of events that no one saw coming, the Gloperjocker has escaped its prison and now roams free. We can no longer locate it.
